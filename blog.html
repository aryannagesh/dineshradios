Dewas has long, hot summers — sometimes 7 to 8 months of AC usage per year. An inverter AC uses up to 50% less electricity compared to a non-inverter AC. This means lower electricity bills and better cooling performance for Dewas's climate.

How to Choose AC Tonnage for Your Dewas Home

  • Small bedroom (up to 120 sq ft): 1 Ton AC is sufficient
  • Medium bedroom (120–180 sq ft): 1.5 Ton inverter AC recommended
  • Large bedroom or hall (180–250 sq ft): 2 Ton AC required
  • Top floor or west-facing room: Add 0.5 Ton for Dewas heat
